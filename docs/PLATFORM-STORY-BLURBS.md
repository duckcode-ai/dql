## One-paragraph platform story
*(Use this as the opening of every community post, email pitch, or article intro)*

> We built the DuckCode Analytics Platform — three open-source tools that give dbt teams a governed path from raw model to trusted AI answer. **DataLex** scans your existing dbt project, uses AI to propose business domain contracts, and lets your team review and certify them before anything becomes trusted. Those certified contracts flow into **dbt** — which stays your source of truth for SQL, tests, and metrics — and then into **DQL**, a local-first analytics workspace where every block, dashboard, and AI answer is pinned to a certified contract. The result: AI answers your analysts can actually trust, with a full lineage trail from source table to response. Works with Snowflake, Databricks, and DuckDB. All open source.

## The "dbt native" angle
*(For dbt Slack, dbt-focused articles — emphasize non-destructive)*

> DataLex and DQL are non-destructive on top of dbt. dbt stays your source of truth — we don't rewrite your models, your YAML, or your tests. DataLex reads your manifest and adds a business domain layer above it. DQL reads your dbt project and builds certified analytics blocks on top of your models. You get governance and governed AI answers without a migration.
