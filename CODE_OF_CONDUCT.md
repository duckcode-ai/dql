# Code of Conduct

We expect contributors to engage respectfully, focus on technical substance, and avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear and factual.
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas rigorously.
- Keep feedback actionable and specific.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove content, or block participation when necessary to keep the project usable for contributors.
